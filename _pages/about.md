I am a PhD student at ENS Paris-Saclay since September 2018 under the supervision of [Vianney Perchet](https://sites.google.com/site/vianneyperchet/). My PhD title is "Statistical Learning in a strategical environment". I am interested in Statistical Machine Learning and Game Theory. Especially, my current topics of research are Multiplayer Multi-Armed bandits and Private Learning.

<span style="color:red">**News:**</span> with the recent NeurIPS deadline, we submitted *Private Learning and Regularized Optimal Transport* with Vianney Perchet. We also submitted *A Practical Algorithm for Multiplayer Bandits when Arm Means Vary Among Players* with Emilie Kaufmann, Abbas Mehrabian and Vianney Perchet. It generalizes the SIC-MMAB algorithm to the heterogeneous case, using an improved communication protocol and some new tricks specific to this setting. The papers are both available in the publications tab. 

Also, the code for *Private Learning and Regularized Optimal Transport* and *SIC-MMAB* are now both available on my GitHub.
