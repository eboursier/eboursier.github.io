I am a PhD candidate at ENS Paris-Saclay since September 2018 under the supervision of Vianney Perchet, at ENS Paris-Saclay. My PhD title is "Statistical Learning in a strategical environment". I am interested in Statistical Machine Learning and Game Theory. Especially, my main topics of interest are Multi-Armed bandits and Private Learning.
