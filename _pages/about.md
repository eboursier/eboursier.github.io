I am a PhD student at ENS Paris-Saclay since September 2018 under the supervision of [Vianney Perchet](https://sites.google.com/site/vianneyperchet/), at ENS Paris-Saclay. My PhD title is "Statistical Learning in a strategical environment". I am interested in Statistical Machine Learning and Game Theory. Especially, my current topics of interest are Multiplayer Multi-Armed bandits and Private Learning.
